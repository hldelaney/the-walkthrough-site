Resources & Guides

The conversations that decide the deal, broken down.

Free, practical guides on the rooms that actually make or break a deal — the commission objection, the FSBO and expired-listing calls, the listing presentation, the buyer consultation, cold-call prospecting, open-house follow-up, the price-reduction talk, and the low appraisal. Each one breaks down the script, the objections, and how to drill it until the answer is automatic.

These guides are the theory. The Walkthrough is the reps.

Reading the script is the easy part. Getting it automatic — in your own voice, against a client who pushes back — takes practice. That's what The Walkthrough is for.

The Walkthrough is an AI roleplay app for real estate agents: you run the listing presentation, the commission objection, the FSBO and expired-listing calls, and the renegotiation against an AI client that pushes back like the real one will — then read a forensic debrief on exactly where you lost the room.
