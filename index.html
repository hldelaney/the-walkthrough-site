Why it works.

Real estate is sold on conversation. The CMA gets you in the door. The conversation closes the door behind you. And conversation is the one thing nobody actually practices — you rehearse it for the first time when the client is sitting across from you.

The Walkthrough flips that. The AI personas aren't friendly chatbots — they're calibrated to push: the buyer who keeps asking about the foundation, the seller who won't budge on price, the inspector report that breaks your timeline, the listing presentation where you're suddenly the third interview. You practice under pressure, in your own voice, on your own deal, before the stakes are real.

And the debrief is the actual point. Every drill ends with a structured read of the conversation — where you anchored, where you hedged, where you handed away leverage by accident. Three reps and the showing isn't a cold open. It's the second take of a scene you already worked.
